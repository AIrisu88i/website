Services offered

Everything your midlife health deserves

Every service we offer is designed specifically for women navigating perimenopause, menopause, and beyond.

Hormone Therapy (HRT)

Bioidentical and conventional options. Fully individualized, monitored, and adjusted over time.

Cognitive & Mood Care

Brain fog, anxiety, and depression addressed as the physiological events they are.

Cardiovascular & Bone Health

Proactive screening and preventive care for the risks that accelerate after menopause.

Sleep & Vasomotor Symptoms

Hot flashes, night sweats, and disrupted sleep treated with clinical and lifestyle approaches.

Metabolic Health

Weight changes and metabolic shifts understood in their full hormonal context.

Specialty Lab Testing

Advanced hormone panels, thyroid, adrenal, and cardiometabolic labs — ordered and interpreted by us.
